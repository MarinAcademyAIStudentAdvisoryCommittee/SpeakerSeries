Our Mission

Moving Beyond Hype to Real Understanding

We're students in grades 9–12 exploring how AI shapes teaching, learning, and everyday life at Marin Academy — and beyond. Our goal isn't to debate "AI is good vs. bad," but to think critically and realistically about what's actually happening.

🌍
Environmental & Social Impact
Supply chains, resource extraction, energy & sustainability
⚖️
Policy & Government
How tech intersects with regulation, equity, and democracy
🎓
Careers & Education
Real paths into AI — from MA to the industry and beyond
🚀
Entrepreneurship & Finance
Impact investing, startups, and responsible innovation
"We want to move beyond abstract or future-oriented discussions and instead focus on real-world applications — the day-to-day realities of AI at work, the exciting and challenging aspects, and the ethical and practical questions that come with it."
— The AI Student Advisory Committee, Marin Academy
